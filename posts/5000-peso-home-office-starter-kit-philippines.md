## FAQs

**Q: Is ₱5,000 really enough for a home office?**
Yes — if you already have a laptop or desktop. This list assumes you have a computer and just need the peripherals and setup items around it.

**Q: What's the single best purchase if I only have ₱2,000?**
A better chair. You sit in it all day. Nothing impacts your health and productivity more.

**Q: Should I buy a monitor or a standing desk first?**
Monitor. A second screen dramatically improves productivity for most remote workers. A standing desk is a health upgrade — great, but less immediately impactful than a monitor.
