## Buying Guide: What to Look For

### 1. Lumbar Support
The most important feature. Look for **adjustable** lumbar support — not just a fixed pillow. Your lumbar spine has a natural inward curve, and a good chair should support it at the right height for your body.

### 2. Breathable Material
Critical in the Philippines. Leather and faux leather chairs get hot and sweaty fast. Look for **full mesh backrests** for maximum breathability.

### 3. Armrest Adjustability
Fixed armrests are almost useless. Look for at least height-adjustable armrests. 3D armrests (like on the Sihoo M57) are ideal.

### 4. Seat Height
Your feet should be flat on the floor with knees at 90 degrees. Most chairs adjust between 42–52 cm, which works for most Filipino heights.

### 5. Weight Capacity
Check the rated weight capacity. Most budget chairs support 100–120 kg. The Sihoo M57 and Deli E4929 both support 150 kg.

### 6. Warranty
Always check warranty terms. Sihoo and TTRacing both offer 2-year warranties through their official Philippine stores.
