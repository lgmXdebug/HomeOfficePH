## Buying Guide: What to Look For

### 1. Lumbar Support
The most important feature. Look for **adjustable** lumbar support — not just a fixed curve.

### 2. Seat Height Adjustment
Your feet should be flat on the floor with knees at roughly 90 degrees.

### 3. Armrest Adjustability
Look for at least height-adjustable armrests. 3D armrests like on the Sihoo M57 are ideal.

### 4. Breathable Material
Mesh backrests are much better than leather for the Philippine climate.

### 5. Weight Capacity
Most budget chairs are rated for 100–120 kg. The Sihoo M57 is rated for 130 kg.
