## Room-by-Room Setup Tips for Filipino Homes

### Small Bedroom Office (Under 10sqm)

**Challenge:** Limited space, desk doubles as personal space.

**Solutions:**
- Use a wall-mounted floating shelf above your desk for extra storage
- Under-desk drawer units add storage without taking floor space
- Cable management is critical — nothing makes a small space feel more cramped than visible cables
- Use vertical space — wall organizers, pegboards, floating shelves

### Condo Living Room Office

**Challenge:** Office in shared living space — needs to look presentable at all times.

**Solutions:**
- Invest in a cable management box to hide the power strip
- Choose wood or bamboo organizers that match home furniture
- A desk mat unifies the look and makes the workspace feel intentional
- Get a monitor arm to free up desk surface and reduce visual clutter

### Dedicated Home Office Room

**Challenge:** Easier to organize but tends to accumulate more clutter over time.

**Solutions:**
- Pegboard on the wall for tools, headphones, and accessories
- Label everything — drawer labels, cable labels, folder labels
- Weekly 5-minute desk reset keeps clutter from building up

**Q: What's the single best desk organization upgrade?**

**Q: Where can I buy desk organization products in the Philippines?**

**Q: How do I keep my desk organized long-term?**
The "one in, one out" rule — if something new comes onto your desk, something else has to go. Also do a 5-minute desk reset at the end of every workday.

**Q: Is a pegboard worth it for a small home office?**
Yes — if you have wall space. A pegboard turns unused vertical wall space into highly functional storage for headphones, cables, tools, and accessories.
